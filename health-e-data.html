Simplified Healthcare Oversight and Disease Surveillance Reporting

Government, public health and regional health organizations collect more and more healthcare information from their provider communities for the purposes of healthcare oversight, disease surveillance, and program management. The challenge for providers however is finding a cost-effective and strategic approach to collecting, managing, analyzing and reporting this vital data.

Health-e-Data Delivers to Public Health Registries

Health-e-Link’s Health-e-Data module is a totally new tool in data management that provides easy-to-use, standards-based templates to deliver your clinical data registry to selected public health agencies and partners.

Cost-effective solution to collecting, managing, analyzing and reporting important data.

Health-e-Link’s Health-e-Data module is designed to help RHIOs, physicians, community-based clinics and hospitals, by enabling healthcare providers to automatically share relevant patient information with local and federal healthcare oversight agencies. Health-e-Data is a web portal using continuity of care, personal health records, and electronic health record standards as a foundation. By incorporating the most current standards for EHRs and CCRs, Health-e-Link is committed to keeping clients up-to-date with emerging health information technology standards.

Through the Health-e-Data portal, providers can easily upload, review, and analyze essential data to:

  • Provide coordinated care for chronically ill patients, with access to advanced directives;
  • Monitor HIV/AIDS and other infectious disease trends;
  • Prevent the spread of infectious diseases and possible pandemics;
  • Identify and treat patients with high risk factors;
  • Share patient immunization records;
  • Communicate family planning information and care plans.

Preserve Funding with Automatic Submission to Healthcare Oversight Agencies

Health-e-Link’s Health-e-Data seamlessly submits the required patient information to customer-defined registries. Select the templates that make sense for your data management needs and let Health-e-Data deliver your clinical data registry automatically on a pre-determined schedule. Submissions and reporting are more timely, allowing physicians and agencies to identify AND treat immediate health threats in your community. With more timely and accurate reporting, agencies can more confidently secure essential federal funding to support these public health initiatives.
