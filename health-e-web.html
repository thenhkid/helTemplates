Health-e-Link Health-e-Web Meets HIPAA Requirements for Secure Email Communication

Share patient information seamlessly and securely with physicians, providers and payers

Health-e-Link Health-e-Web module provides a secure email communication solution that spans the virtual boundaries between selected healthcare partners in your community. Using state-of-the art, email encryption and authentication procedures, Health-e-Web provides a web-based secure network for transmitting correspondence, clinical notes and reports, audio files, images and more to anywhere you can access an email address.

Health-e-Web is a secure email communication solution for sharing clinical records between your network of physicians, providers and payers. RererralLink allows you to create and share secure email communications that meets the privacy and security concerns for each partner in your community using a pre-defined set of HIPAA compliant rules. Use Health-e-Web to securely:

  • Meet all HIPAA standards for safeguarding patient privacy for storing, transferring and accessing healthcare data.
  • Implement a low-overhead email encryption solution that requires minimal resources to implement and maintain;
  • Send secure, encrypted emails that meet a variety of patient communication needs;
  • Obtain referrals and authorizations for medical services;
  • Enhance physician-to-physician collaboration to develop more thorough care plans with smoother referrals;
  • Provide superior anti-virus and anti-spam protection
